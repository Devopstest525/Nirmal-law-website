about us

Law Firm Devoted to Our Clients

Adv. Prabhat Kumar Singh is a Ranchi-based advocate with primary practice in the Jharkhand High Court. A graduate of the Faculty of Law, University of Delhi, he combines academic excellence with practical legal insight to provide effective legal solutions. His practice areas include constitutional law, civil and criminal litigation, service matters, and writ jurisdictions.

We are committed to delivering sharp, strategic, and result-oriented legal solutions. Specializing in matters relating to freezing and unfreezing of bank accounts, financial fraud, and enforcement actions, we provide expert consultation and representation for individuals and businesses facing regulatory or procedural hurdles.

Our practice is built on trust, confidentiality, and in-depth legal insight, particularly in navigating complex issues involving banks, financial institutions, and enforcement agencies. Whether you're seeking pre-litigation advice or strong courtroom representation, our office ensures a professional approach tailored to each client's unique needs.
